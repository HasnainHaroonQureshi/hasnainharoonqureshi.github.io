★ Final Year Project

Deep Learning–Based Structural Crack Detection, Segmentation, Width Estimation & Severity Classification

Model: YOLOv11s-seg · Sep 2025 – May 2026

A complete AI-powered structural health monitoring system that detects cracks in concrete infrastructure, segments their geometry, estimates physical crack width, and classifies damage severity — all from a single image captured on a smartphone.

  • Instance segmentation with YOLOv11s-seg
  • Real-time crack width estimation pipeline
  • Severity classification (hairline → critical)
  • Web platform for desktop inspection
  • Android app for on-site field deployment
